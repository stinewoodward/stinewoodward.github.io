Breweries in North America and Hawaii, 2017

A hexbin map that aggregates breweries by hexagons of 50km in diameter. Map made using QGIS and SQL for data processing. Data from the POI Factory and Natural Earth.

The Appalachian Trail on Raisz (1957)

A special commemorative map of the Appalachian Trail with a classic Erwin Raisz map as base. Raisz basemap was georeferenced in QGIS. Raisz map from the Raisz estate, Appalachian trail layer provided by the Appalachian Trail Conservancy.

US Wildfires, 2013

This map shows how many cumulative acres were burned by wildfires in each U.S. county in 2013. Also shown are the location, name, and relevant information for individual fires. I made the map using SQL and point-polygon analysis. Data from Karen Short of the U.S. Fire Analysis Program.

Cancer Death Rates

This map shows cancer death rates (deaths per 100,000 people) for all U.S. counties. Data set manipulated in QGIS, map made with CARTO. The data describe cancer rates for all races, sexes, and ages, averaged over the years 2009-2013. Data courtesy of National Cancer Institute.

Hometown: Ridgefield, WA

This map shows points of interests in downtown Ridgefield, WA. I went to high school in this town. Long live the fightin' Spuds. Map coded in JS.

Median Rent by US County, 2015

This map shows the median rent for all U.S. counties in 2015. Map coded using leaflet. Rent data are from the U.S. Census Bureau.

Gender Equity in Kenya, 2014

This proportional symbol map displays enrollment by gender and grade level in Kenya counties for 2014. Map coded using mapbox.js, assembly.css, and jquery-sparkline.js. Data courtesy of the Kenya National Bureau of Statistics.

Affordable Housing in Portland, OR

This choropleth map explores affordable housing in Portland, OR through a variety of housing-related variables. The map is coded using D3.js and leaflet. Data courtesy of the US Census Bureau and the Portland State University Urban Displacement project.

Vegetation of Coastal Louisiana, 2013

This thematic map shows vegetation types on the Lousiana Coast in 2013. Data processed using Mapshaper at the command line. Map created in Mapbox Studio by overlaying data over a custom tile set. Data courtesy of the U.S. Geological Survey.

Property Damage by Storms in the US, 2017

This map of storm property damage in the USA starts as a heat map that transitions to a dot map as you zoom in. Map coded using Mapbox GL JS. Data courtesy of the NOAA Storm Events Database.

Seattle Traffic Camera Locations, 2012

I collaborated with Andrea Parr to make this cluster marker map of traffic camera locations in Seattle, WA. Map coded with Leaflet, including markercluster plugin. Data courtesy of the City of Seattle Open Data Portal.
